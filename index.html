Cherry is the only child to House Lucero, a minor noble family in South Faerghus. House Lucero, similarly to House Daphnel, hasn't had a crest-bearing heir for several generations, contributing to their decline. As a result, when Cherry was born, their parents were disappointed by their lack of a crest.

Their parents treat them coldly and leave the care-taking to their servants ‚Äî the Hidalgo family. The Hidalgo's are a family of commoners serving House Lucero. The parents look after Cherry, while their son and daughter like to play with them.

Eventually fed up with the Lucero's idea of "family", the Hidalgo's make plans to leave South Faerghus with Cherry. Cherry's parents don't stop them for leaving, nor do they bother looking for their child for the next 20 years. The Hidalgo's change their last name from Hidalgo to Perrineau, and settle in Gloucester Territory.

Sometime during the war, Cherry receives a letter from their parents. After failing to bear any more children, House Lucero seeks out the daughter they gave away and demands them back. They are desperate to have an heir ‚Äî even a crestless one ‚Äî to continue their lineage. Cherry returns to their now unfamiliar family estate in South Faerghus to confront their birth parents.

Entering a dilapidated castle, Cherry and their classmates must defeat hired bandits to reach the main room where their parents reside. They pass by portraits veiled in dust, echoes of a past long gone. Faces of their parents, and those who came before them, watch them as they make their way to the main room.

Not a single portrait of their daughter lines the faded walls.

Pushing open the doors into the main room,Cherry finally stands before their parents. Their parents, enthusiastic to see them, reach into their pockets and offer them a gift ‚Äî a Crest Stone. They've spent the last of their gold trying to procure it, confident that its power could fix their daughter.

Turning away, Cherry declines the gift. The life of a commoner is kinder than the one their parents offer. Enraged, their parents move to attack Cherry. However, the Crest Stone transforms one of them into a Giant Wolf. The Demonic Beast kills the other parent before turning on Cherry's house.

House Lucero falls. After defeating the Giant Wolf, Cherry continues to live happily as a commoner with the Perrineau's.

After the battle of Garreg Mach, Cherry returns to their home in Gloucester. They continue to help their parents out at the inn. Cherry is tasked with cooking and gathering ingredients. The latter has contributed to their rather hectic life ‚Äî after having accidentally saved several townsfolk from Wild Beasts on their hunts, they became known as the "Red Caped Savior".

Not many of their classmates realize this. Their ditsy personality is a far cry from their heroic image. Some people, like Claude, like to use their new epithet to poke fun at them.

After the war ends, they either continue to stay with their family or set out to explore the world.
